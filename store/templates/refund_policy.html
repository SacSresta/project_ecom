Refund Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

At Basana Boutique, we strive to provide the best possible shopping experience to all of our customers. If you are unhappy with your purchase for any reason, we gladly accept returns under the following policies:

1. Return Policy

  • We have a 30-day return policy from the date of receiving your item.
  • Only full-priced items are eligible for returns. Sale items and discounted items are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

2. Fees and Responsibilities

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
  • Store credits and refunds are processed only after we receive the items in unused and original condition.
  • Initial delivery costs are non-refundable.

3. How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please contact us at basanaofficial2024@gmail.com. If your return is accepted, we will send you instructions on how and where to send your package.

4. Damages and Issues

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged, or if you received the wrong item.

  • For damage during transit, please provide clear pictures of the damaged contents and packaging.
  • Claims for lost items must be made within 3 days of delivery.
  • We will issue a full refund, including shipping, once any investigation is completed (2-6 weeks).

5. Manufacturing Defects

For items with manufacturing defects, we will replace the damaged items or issue a full refund, including shipping charges, once we receive the items in their original condition.

6. Handmade Items Disclaimer

Handmade items may differ slightly in size, color, and weight. These variations should be seen as a unique character rather than a defect.

Thank you for choosing Basana Boutique. We are committed to your satisfaction and look forward to providing you with beautiful, high-quality South Asian fashion.
